LegalLens AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

LegalLens AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

LegalLens AI collects the following types of information:

  • Text Content: When you use our extension to analyze privacy policies or terms of service, we process the text you provide or that is extracted from webpages.
  • Questions: Questions you ask about privacy policies or terms of service.
  • Usage History: We store your analysis and question history locally on your device to provide the history feature.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Analyze privacy policies and terms of service to provide you with insights.
  • Answer your questions about privacy-related matters.
  • Improve our extension's functionality and user experience.
  • Maintain a history of your analyses and questions for your convenience.

Data Storage and Security

LegalLens AI stores your analysis history and cached results locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data does not leave your device except when being processed by our API.

When text is sent to our API for analysis, it is transmitted securely using HTTPS. We do not permanently store the content of your privacy policies or questions on our servers beyond what's necessary to process your request.

Third-Party Services

LegalLens AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Hugging Face: We use Hugging Face's API to process and analyze text. Please refer to Hugging Face's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local history and cached data through the extension's interface.
  • Uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
